About the Site

Robux Tax O'Calc is a small test project by Devin Lin. It was created to be used as an easy way to calculate Roblox Sales Tax. Some people might not know this, but Roblox has a 30% sales tax (creator gets 70%) on most of its items.

This started as a Web Dev test in late April 2022. This site uses the languages HTML (Hyper Text Markup Language), CSS (Cascading Style Sheets), and JS (Javascript).

About Me

I'm Devin Lin, and I have been playing Roblox since 2017. Over the years, I have dealt with transfering around Robux from and to different accounts and groups.

Extremely recently, I have been learning web development. Since Robux transfers on Roblox has to go through Roblox Sales Tax. I thought it would've been the perfect thing to create an Online Roblox Sales Tax Calculator.
